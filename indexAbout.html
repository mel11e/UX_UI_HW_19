    • About Me

    Hi, I’m Melissa Garcia.

    I am a UX/UI Designer with an intrinsic ability to see connections where others don’t to solve user problems and improve lives through UX/UI research and design to capture user needs in order to deliver solutions.

    I’m a life learner, fascinated by the way things are designed and knowing why things work the way they do, or don’t work.

    I am experienced in desktop and mobile design with skills in Adobe XD, Figma, Invision, Illustrator, etc. I have degrees in biology and psychology. More recently I obtained a certification in UX/UI Design from Rice University and successfully completed three case study projects. Case studies included: designing a mobile app for art enthusiasts to quickly find local events and art related activities, redesigned a government agency (mobile and desktop), and a local Houston non-profit nature conservation website (mobile and desktop).
